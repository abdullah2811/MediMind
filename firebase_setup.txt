1. Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/project/medimind-368ed/authentication

2. Click "Get started" under Authentication if prompted.

3. Click the "Sign-in method" tab.

4. Enable these providers:

   - Phone -> Enable
   - Google -> Enable (add support email if asked)

5. Then go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/project/medimind-368ed/settings/general/

6. Under "Your apps" -> Android app (com.example.medimind)
